Shell redesign - 1:1 mockup

Everything in the bottom panel is the proposed design, built from the real Tora tokens. The wallpaper and this card are mockup chrome.

1Sidebar = categories only. Library filters and brand. Boards and Settings move out.
2Boards as pills above the deck: History first, then boards, then +. Scrolls horizontally.
3Settings gear next to the Panel / Window switcher, top right.
4Sidebar collapses to an icon rail (60px), never fully hides. Toggle at the rail's foot.
5Two-layer background: the frame (sidebar, bars) on a light glass tint; the items area in its own deeper, inset well.
Fonts fall back to system here; the app uses Space Grotesk + Hanken Grotesk. Try the search icon, the pills, and the sidebar rows - they are clickable.
